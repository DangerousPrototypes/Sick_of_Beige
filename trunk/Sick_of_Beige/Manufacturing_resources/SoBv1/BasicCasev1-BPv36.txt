Bus Pirate v3.6 acrylic case v1 (DP6037)

A clear laser-cut acrylic case kit for the [Bus Pirate v3.6 http://www.seeedstudio.com/depot/bus-pirate-v3-assembled-p-609.html?cPath=174  ]. This case is designed to be simple, cheap, and easy to assemble. The Bus Pirate PCB is sandwiched between two laser-cut acrylic sheets, and the whole assembly is held together with aluminum screws and standoffs.

This is only compatible with Bus Pirate v3.6 that uses a DP6037 standard PCB template.
